Private Lessons

Get personalized instruction with our 45-minute private lessons, designed for dancers of all levels-from complete beginners to experienced competitors.

Work one-on-one with our qualified and experienced instructors, who will tailor each lesson to your goals, whether you want to build confidence, refine technique, or master a new dance style.

Private lessons offer the fastest way to improve, helping you progress at your own pace with individualized attention. Ready to take your dancing to the next level? Book your private lesson today!

Group Lessons

Our 45-minute group classes are a fun and affordable way to learn to dance while meeting new people!

With multiple skill levels available, you'll find the right class to match your experience, whether you're a beginner or a seasoned dancer. Classes are held in a private room, creating a comfortable and focused learning environment.

Group lessons are a great way to build confidence, improve technique, and enjoy the energy of dancing with others. Join us for a class and let's get moving!

Wedding Dance Lessons

Make your first dance unforgettable with a wedding dance tailored to your vision!

Whether you want simple, elegant steps or a fully choreographed routine with dips and tricks, our experienced instructors will guide you every step of the way.

We offer a variety of lesson options to help make your special day even more memorable.
